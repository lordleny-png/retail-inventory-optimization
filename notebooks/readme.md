# Retail Inventory Optimization-Business Analyst Project
## Business Context 
This project  focuses on a retail store selling daily consumer product. The store experiences frequent stock-outs in the middle of the month,leading to lost sales and insufficient profit.
##Business Problem 
The retail business frequently runs out of stocks before the end of the month. This poor inventory management causes lost sales opportunities and negatively affect profitability.
## Project Objectives
-Analyze sales and inventory patterns to identify inefficiencies 
-Propose data-driven stock optimization actions to reduce stock-outs and improve profit
